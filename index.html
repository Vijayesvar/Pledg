    Frequently Asked Questions

    Everything you need to know about our Bitcoin-backed loans

    What is the LTV ratio and why is 50% beneficial?

    expand_more

    LTV (Loan-to-Value) ratio is the proportion of the loan amount to the value of your collateral. Our 50% LTV means you can borrow up to 50% of your Bitcoin's value. This balanced ratio offers protection against market volatility while still providing substantial liquidity for your needs.

    How does the Fireblocks MPC wallet integration work?

    expand_more

    Fireblocks' Multi-Party Computation (MPC) technology distributes the cryptographic keys across multiple parties, eliminating single points of failure. This means your collateral is protected by institutional-grade security without relying on a single private key that could be compromised.

    What happens if the value of Bitcoin drops?

    expand_more

    If Bitcoin's value decreases, your LTV ratio increases. At certain thresholds, you may receive margin calls to either add more collateral or partially repay your loan to maintain a healthy LTV. Our 50% initial LTV provides a substantial buffer against market volatility.

    What disbursement methods do you support?

    expand_more

    We currently disburse loans in INR via bank transfer and in USDT stablecoins. Additional options may be added in the future based on demand and regulatory considerations.

    What are the loan terms and interest rates?

    expand_more

    Loan terms range from 3 to 36 months. Interest rates start from 8.5% APR and vary by term, loan amount, and market conditions. You will see your final rate before accepting.

    Do you rehypothecate collateral?

    expand_more

    No. Pledg does not rehypothecate collateral. Your BTC is segregated and never lent or pledged elsewhere.

    Can I monitor my collateral on-chain?

    expand_more

    Yes. We provide an on-chain address so you can monitor your collateral at all times for transparency.

    How fast can I get funded?

    expand_more

    Once collateral is received and verification is complete, disbursement typically occurs within 24 hours to INR bank accounts or within minutes for USDT transfers.

    Are there prepayment penalties?

    expand_more

    No. You can repay early at any time. Interest accrues only for days outstanding.

    What are the eligibility and KYC requirements?

    expand_more

    Standard KYC and AML checks are required before disbursement. Availability may vary by jurisdiction.

    How is my BTC stored?

    expand_more

    Collateral is held using MPC wallets with multi-party approvals and policy controls. Keys are split and never exist in one place.
